OUR STORY

Historically it is considered to be date of foundation of the company 1954 when in South Korea Hadonghwan Motor Company was organized. First the company was engaged in release of Jeep cars for the American army.

Active opposition between northern and southern parts of the country resulted in rather great demand for these cars that, certainly, favored to its development. It allowed the company to start releasing also civil cars, including — for export. For example, in 1967 Hadonghwan delivered the buses to Vietnam.
